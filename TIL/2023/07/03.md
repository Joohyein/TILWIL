문제 : Weather Observation Station 5   
STATION에서 가장 짧고 긴 CITY의 이름과 각각의 길이 찾기

```sql
    SELECT CITY, LENGTH(CITY)
    FROM STATION
    ORDER BY LENGTH(CITY), CITY
    LIMIT 1;

    SELECT CITY, LENGTH(CITY)
    FROM STATION
    ORDER BY LENGTH(CITY) DESC, CITY DESC
    LIMIT 1;
```
